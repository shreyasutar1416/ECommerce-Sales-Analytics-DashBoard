# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides comprehensive insights into e-commerce sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, product categories, and payment methods. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor key metrics and make data-driven decisions.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing states and customers.
- Monitor monthly profit and loss trends.
- Evaluate product category contributions.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Discover the most profitable product sub-categories.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales by State
- Visualizes sales distribution across different states.
- Helps identify high-performing regions.

### 2. Quantity by Category
- Displays contribution of product categories:
  - Clothing
  - Electronics
  - Furniture

### 3. Profit/Loss by Month
- Tracks monthly profitability trends.
- Highlights profitable and loss-making periods.

### 4. Sales by Customer
- Identifies top customers based on purchase amount.

### 5. Quantity by Payment Mode
- Analyzes customer payment preferences:
  - Cash on Delivery (COD)
  - UPI
  - Debit Card
  - Credit Card
  - EMI

### 6. Profit by Sub-Category
- Shows the most profitable product sub-categories:
  - Printers
  - Bookcases
  - Sarees
  - Accessories
  - Tables

### 7. Quarter-wise Filtering
- Interactive slicers for:
  - Q1
  - Q2
  - Q3
  - Q4

## 🔍 Insights Generated

- Maharashtra generated the highest sales revenue.
- Clothing category contributed the largest share of sales quantity.
- COD was the most preferred payment mode.
- Printers generated the highest profit among all sub-categories.
- Profitability fluctuated across months with losses observed during certain periods.
